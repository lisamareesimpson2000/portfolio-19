Marlborough Escape

Tourism New Zealand works with the tourism industry to positively promote New Zealand Internationally. I created a web application for accommodation based in the Marlborough sounds and targeting visitors that are interested in doing the Queen Charlotte Track. The website allows travelers to find their own accommodation and select how many people are in their party and how long they will be staying. There are also meal options to choose from. This website is a helpful tool for visitors to New Zealand to work out how much it will cost them.

Comes and Goes

Comes and Goes brings an explosion of edible flowers and culinary masterpieces that Wellingtonians are enjoying. Its clean, minimalist look and ‘gram-worthy’ menu have attracted clientele from all over the Wellington region. I have created an online menu to help create excellent customer engagement and brand awareness as they currently don't have a website. This project is created for educational purposes, designed and developed by Lisa Simpson.

Weatherwise

There is something about knowing the weather that is comforting... There are very few instances in which we can know a little bit about what the future will be like. Weather is one of those things where someone can predict what's to come and be right. Having that kind of stability about the scary unknown is nice. Weatherwise is a contemporary App which brings you more than the daily forecast. Minimilistic design is paired with coherent data to bring a unique and powerful user-interface to your disposal.

WeatherWise is a collaborative student project designed and developed by Michael Parker, Hannah Wehipeihana and Lisa Simpson @ Yoobee School of Design.
